1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when registering for DevConf 2026. This includes your name, email address, job title, company name, GitHub profile (optional), and payment processing details. We do not store credit card numbers on our servers; all payments are handled securely via third-party processors.

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to issue your conference badges, provide access to digital streaming portals, send event updates, and improve your overall experience. With your explicit consent, we may also share relevant news regarding hackathons, workshop materials, and post-event highlights.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your information may only be shared with verified event partners or sponsors if you allow them to scan your attendee badge at their physical booths or explicitly join their recruiting pipeline during the conference.

4. Data Security

We implement robust administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. Access to your personal account data is restricted strictly to team members who require it for operational purposes.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (such as the EU under GDPR), you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of the personal data we hold about you. If you wish to update your profile or withdraw your consent from our mailing list, please contact our support desk.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses standard cookies and session tracking pixels to optimize your browsing speed, remember your ticket preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser settings, though certain parts of the ticketing portal may stop functioning properly.
